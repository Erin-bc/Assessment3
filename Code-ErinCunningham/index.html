TAN VIVO

Tan Vivo was created as a reaction to the overload of apartment buildings which follow a minimalist and luxury style branding. While this can be a method to prove prestige, it often comes across as quite cold, sterile and lacks life. Tan Vivo translated from Spanish means ‘So Alive’, the name and the brand both reflect an authentic life, one which is vibrant and adventurous.

TAPPER ST. HONEY

Tapper Street Honey is a brand which is built on the nostalgic, story-book perspective of honey. The brand draws back to the golden age of children's literature, with inspiration coming from A. A. Milne’s Winnie-the-Pooh and the shared feeling of nostalgia it creates across multiple generations.

The High Street Hive is an extension of the brand which provides the full brand experience in the form of a cafe. The High Street Hive is a specialty cafe which specialises in tea and honey pairings with a high tea style menu.

The Tapper Street Honey brand visuals uphold a balance between childishness and sophistication. The textured and colourful illustration paired with the story-book word-mark creates a feeling of nostalgia whilst still maintaining a premium appearance.

NOONGAR CALENDAR

The Noongar seasons (unsurprisingly) have more specific indicators of the change in seasons. With often quite beautiful indicators, like the presence of dew in the early mornings. An indicator of the start of Djeran, the season of April and May.

This should be common knowledge for people living in Noongar boodja, not only to acknowledge the traditional custodians of the land, but to also have a more accurate understanding of the environment in which we live

VENOSA JEWELLERY

“Love doesn’t need to be perfect. It just needs to be true.” No perfect diamond can represent true love, and thats where Venosa jewellery started. The purpose of Venosa jewellery is to create life long pieces that hold true representation of genuine and imperfect love.

Venosa jewellery celebrates the beautiful imperfections of human connection. All Venosa pieces are hand crafted, each piece with natural maker’s marks which make them unique. As a reflection of the brand’s positioning, the visuals are textural, with rough edges and a slightly imperfect appearance.

LEWIS CROMEY PINSTRIPING

Lewis grew up fixing vintage cars with his Dad. Through this, the aesthetics of the 50s and 60s and the art of pinstriping became a keen interest. Lewis is the only pinstriping specialist (and owner of the most beautiful ‘59 Parkwood station wagon) in the greater Perth region.

The branding I designed for Lewis captures his cheeky and down-to-earth personality, while also subtly alluding to the aesthetics of the 50s and 60s. The youthful design of Lewis Cromey Pinstriping brings a renaissance to the rare art of pinstriping.

TCM ENGINES
